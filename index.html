The Ongoing Struggle

A Century of Progress

For over a century, the fight for equality has transformed societies, challenged norms, and redefined what it means to be human in the modern world.

From the suffragette movements of the early 1900s to today's global advocacy for LGBTQ+ rights and gender equality, millions have fought for the simple right to exist, to vote, to work, and to love freely.

Yet in 2026, the journey continues. While some nations have made remarkable strides, others still have significant progress to make. This archive documents where we stand today.
